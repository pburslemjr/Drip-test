1 Requirements, Company Knowledge and Design Rationale

1.1 Company Knowledge

Company knowledge was summarized in the CompanyKnowledge module. It contains a sensor database, a database of warning systems and knowledge about vehicle stopping performance.

The sensors database contains two senzors - Fig. 1.1-A. Each of them is represented by three parameters - detection range, sensor cost and time to market. The first sensor can detect an obstacle in higher distance and it could be sooner available for sale, however it is more expensive compared to the second sensor.

Figure 1.1-A: Senzors from the company database - SensData.

The database of warning systems consists of three warning systems (bellow as text). They are represented by two parameters - driver reaction time and warning system cost. The first warning system assists a driver to react the soonest, however it is the most expensive. The third warning system is the cheapest, however a driver will react at the latest. The second warning system is in the middle in both characteristics.

1.2.1 A New Initiative to implement a Low Cost Collision Warning System (CWS) (ER1) [functional]

A new initiative is being rolled out to introduce a low cost Collision Warning System (CWS). The CWS will provide a warning to the driver when there is an obseved danger of collision, so that the driver can react appropriately and avoid the collision.

The CWS will be a passive system, and will not interfere with the vehicle performance directly.

1.2.1.1 Collision Avoidance (ER1CA) [functional]

In order to avoid a collision, we need the stopping distance to be smaller than the distance at which the obstacle was detected.

1.2.1.2 Cost (ER1Cost) [functional]

A critical business requirement to make this initiative successful is that the cost of the overall system be kept to acceptable limits. The overall system cost is considered in terms of three major constituents: The cost of the sensing system, the cost of the warning system, and the equivalent cost associated with time-to-market related to any solution.

1.2.1.3 Safe distance (DistReq) [functional]

Min distance from target to subject vehicle should be greater than 3 m for all possible speed conditions

1.2.1.4 Vehicle Platforms (VehPlatforms) [functional]

The applicable vehicle platforms are only those already in production.

1.2.1.5 No New Parts Sourcing (NoNewSourcing) [functional]

In order to keep the product management under control, there is a requirement that no new parts will be sourced. This implies existing components need to be used.

1.2.1.6 Road Conditions (rdCons) [functional]

The vehicle is considered to travel on roads where the friciton coefficient between the tyre and the road varies from 0.4 (wet roads) to 0.9 (dry roads)

1.2.1.7 Deceleration Range (decelRange) [functional]

For comfortable and jerk-free deceleration during braking, the deceleration of the car should be less than 0.5 g
